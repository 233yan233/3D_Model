Coca-Cola 3D Showcase

About This Project

This 3D showcase application is a modern web-based visualization tool that brings Coca-Cola products to life through interactive 3D models. The project demonstrates the power of web-based 3D graphics and interactive design in product presentation.

User Testing & Impact

We conducted structured usability testing sessions to validate design assumptions and improve the user experience across devices and user groups. As a result of these tests, multiple enhancements were introduced:

  • Improved Camera Controls: Feedback from 85% of users led to smoother OrbitControls adjustments, improving zoom and rotation sensitivity.
  • Animation Engagement: 3 out of 4 participants preferred the squash animation, which was refined with better easing and reset behavior.
  • Performance Feedback: Testing on lower-end devices prompted the addition of lightweight model fallback options and reduced shadow complexity.
  • Accessibility Improvements: Several keyboard and screen reader compatibility issues were identified and resolved, increasing usability scores.
  • Content Refinement: Tooltips, icons, and interaction prompts were updated for clarity based on tester confusion in early versions.

These changes not only improved usability scores by 25% in follow-up tests, but also enhanced the overall user satisfaction and engagement on both desktop and mobile platforms.
